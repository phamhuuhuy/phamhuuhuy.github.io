Project Idea

This part shows my web based project idea, to be more specific, this is Advanced Transportation Training Driving School Management System, Directions and Update Traffic Situation which is called Supporting Transportation Apps. It can support for people who are driving and want to know about the unpleasant driving experiences such as weather changes, accidents, or congestion. Analyzing the car speed, suggesting the suitable speed to avoid exceeding the allowed speed and the shortest and the fastest way to destination. Moreover, people can sign up for training driving school and booking vehicles or instructors for training. This can prevent the efforts of human power and wealth, additionally, make sure that driving-training information can be utilized effectively.

Outcome

If my project is successful, reducing accidents and raising awareness when people participate in traffic will be the outcome. Money is also one result I think about because many people will install and download my app on their devices. Although Supporting Transportation App has a range of benefits, there are some negative sides as well as problems when we create this project. We must guarantee that every account is secure and money in it is not taken by hacker or penetration. Therefore, we need to create the security team who can protect user’s information and check the operation of app. From my perspective, this development will have a huge amount of impact in the world, especially in Vietnam. This is a country that is one of the highest death rates in the world due to traffic accidents.
